We are beautiful is a project by Eternal Gratidude... OU, incorporated in Tallinn, Estonia.

What is the with the silly company name? It is an inside joke that stemmed from a recent gathering of our tribe. Someone was looking for a ride to the event and posted:

You will get eternal gratitude and maybe a white russian.

It was a point of great joy and laughter for us that weekend and when we decided to start the company and needed a name for it, the name was clear.

Given that our site has a much more realistic name, we decided to have fun with the actual company name. As you can tell, we love humor and being sily; we do not take ourselves too seriously.
